📋 Methodology

How savings are calculated:

The average cost of a denied dental insurance claim — including administrative time to resubmit, patient communication, delayed payment, and write-offs — is estimated at ${{ avg_denial_cost }} per claim based on industry research from the American Dental Association and dental practice management studies.

Estimated savings = (Pre-Auth Alerts + Documentation Alerts) × ${{ avg_denial_cost }} average denial cost

Note: This is a conservative estimate. Actual savings may be higher when accounting for procedures that would have been written off rather than resubmitted.
